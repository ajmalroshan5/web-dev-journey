FAVOURITE INDIAN MOVIES OF AJMAL

My Top3 Fav Movies

Hey Ram (Tamil)

When his wife, Aparna, gets raped during the partition of India, Saketh Ram joins a gang that aims to kill Mahatma Gandhi because he advocates peace with Muslims.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara (Hindi)

Friends Kabir, Imran and Arjun take a vacation in Spain before Kabir's marriage. The trip turns into an opportunity to mend fences, heal wounds, fall in love with life and combat their worst fears

Gangs of Wasseypur (2 Parts)

"Gangs of Wasseypur" is a 2012 Indian Hindi-language film directed by Anurag Kashyap. It is an epic black comedy crime film that explores themes of gang wars, crime, revenge, and the continuance of hatred across generations, spanning over seven decades.
